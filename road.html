TOL

Little red bird is leading the way. You are not far behind, he has been good about keeping a sustainable pace. Odd thing though, now that you are not touching the tree, the loneliness is sinking back in. The further the bird flies from you, the more isolated you feel. Nothing feels right. You have lost your connection to everything, and the only way to get it back, seems to be to touch a living thing. You are in the middle of a thought, you’re thinking about how you will not be leaving this experience a good yelp review when you hear a tiny little clinking. The clinking was akin to a pin drop in a quiet room, you would have never heard it if even a small wind had been making its way through, but ‘clink clink clink’ you hear. The bird is a little ways ahead of you but when he notices that you’ve slowed he (in frustrated movements only a bird could make) flies back towards you to see what the hold up is. ‘I hear something’ you say. ‘ I want to see what it is, maybe it is another person lost like me’ the bird settles on a tree branch at about shoulder height to you. He hangs his head and lets out a sigh, ‘well let’s get on with it then’
