TOL

He looks at you but only for a moment, then he quickly goes back to pawing the ornament. ‘clink’ ‘clink’ ‘clink’ it goes. He seems focused and determined. You ask him, why are you making so much noise? To which he replies “I am lost. I have lost my human and I have lost my memory. I do not know where I am, but I am hungry. I know that whenever I hear this sound, food appears, it is but a very vague memory, but the instinct is strong. Why is it not working?” This cat is familiar. You know this man. As soon as you pet his head, it all comes back to you. This is YOUR CAT! What are we doing here, how do we get back?! And WHERE ARE MY MEMORIES?
