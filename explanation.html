TOL

The little red bird is standing on the ground now, keeping a cautious distance from the cat. His red color is a beautiful contrast to the untouched white snow all around him. He looks up at you but then puts his head immediately down again as if he was going to say something, but couldn’t. ‘What is it’ you urge. He begins, still looking at the ground ‘you are in the woods, my friend, but not just any woods. These woods get lonely, but when they do, don’t hesitate to reach out and touch those around you that care for you and love you. Don’t ever think you’re in these woods alone, you may not see any footprints in the snow, but don’t make assumptions. I am here, but you don’t see my footprints because I fly. Some days, you will wake up in these woods, and you won’t know why you’re here or how to get out, but you just need to remember to reach out and touch because Whitney, I will always be there for you. Now, if we keep walking, I want to show you a magical trail, a trail that will take you out of the woods if only for a moment. Grab your loved one’s hand and enjoy the journey, don’t forget to touch”
